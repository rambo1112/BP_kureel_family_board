About This Initiative

The Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS), under the Ministry of Earth Sciences, provides critical ocean information and advisory services. These services support disaster risk reduction and maritime safety, covering hazards like tsunamis, storm surges, and high waves to help authorities and coastal communities make informed decisions during emergencies.

India‚Äôs vast coastline is vulnerable to numerous ocean hazards. While agencies like INCOIS provide early warnings using advanced technology, there is often a delay or lack of real-time, on-the-ground reports from citizens. Furthermore, valuable insights from public discussions on social media during these events remain largely untapped. This platform concept addresses the need for a unified system that bridges this gap, enabling citizens to contribute observations and allowing for the analysis of social media trends to build a comprehensive, real-time picture of an evolving hazard event.
